Virtual Reality is a very recent interest of mine, but has hit me full force with how immersive and enjoyable it was. VR has opened up a new window to new possibilities in the future of entertainment and experiences. I believe VR has mnay practicle application. For example, someone may develop an application for doctors to preform surgery on a virtual patient to get more "hands-on" experience, while being able to perceive much more detail versus a plastic training dummy.

Self-driving Vehicles

Self-driving vehicles is clearly the future of transportation. Full self-driving will be exponentially safer in the near future. In addition to safety, the convenience of being transported without having to be constantly vigilant will be an amazing experience. Sure, someone can take public transportation without having to pay attention to the road, but having a personal vehicle that operates around their own schedule and routes would be a great experience.
